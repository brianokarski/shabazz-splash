Shabazz Projects ⓘ

Shabazz Projects is a progressive publishing platform focusing on books and objects with an emphasis on contemporary artists, photography, and design.

We (Hassan Rahim & Brian Okarski) just completed the LA Art Book Fair with our friends Catalogue, who we had the pleasure of sharing a booth with. Catalogue invited us to create the supplement for Library Paper No. 4.

To keep in touch, like us on Facebook, follow us on Soundcloud, or email us.
